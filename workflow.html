Innovating Boundary Delimitation for Morocco and Spain

Our Concept

This project will explore the differences and similarities in land delimitation practices between Spain and Morocco, focusing on key aspects such as legal and technical perspectives. From a legal standpoint, it will examine how each country's framework governs land boundaries, including cadastral systems and dispute resolution mechanisms. Technically, the study will go through the methodologies used and the accuracy obtained through various techniques such as GNSS, photogrammetry, and drone-based surveys identifies strengths and weaknesses in current practices.The historical evolution of land delimitation in both countries is also considered to highlight the influence of cultural and administrative legacies. Based on these findings, the study will extract critical challenges, and proposes innovative solutions. These may include leveraging AI for automated boundary detection..

How We Achieve It

Step 1: Data Collection

Gather orthomosaics, GNSS data, and legal documents to build a comprehensive geospatial database.

Step 2: AI Integration

Develop machine learning models to automate boundary detection and optimize field verification.

Step 3: Geoportal Deployment

Launch an interactive platform for boundary mapping, legal dispute resolution, and collaboration.
